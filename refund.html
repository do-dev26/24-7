Last updated: April 19, 2025

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We stand behind SaleIQ. If you're not completely satisfied within the first 30 days of your paid subscription, we'll give you a full refund — no questions asked.

1. Eligibility for Refund

You are eligible for a full refund if:

  • Your refund request is submitted within 30 days of your first payment
  • This is your first subscription to SaleIQ (not a re-subscription)
  • You contact us at support@saleiq.app with your account email

2. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds are not provided for:

  • Requests made after the 30-day window
  • Accounts terminated for violating our Terms of Service
  • Partial months of service after the 30-day period
  • Annual plan subscriptions after the 30-day refund window
  • Add-on word packages that have been used

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please:

  • Email us at support@saleiq.app with the subject line "Refund Request"
  • Include your account email address and the reason for your request
  • We will process your refund within 5–7 business days
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method

4. Cancellations

You may cancel your subscription at any time from your dashboard under Billing. Cancellation stops future charges. Your account will remain active until the end of the current billing period. No partial refunds are issued for unused subscription time beyond the 30-day window.

5. Trial Period

If you're on a free trial, no charge occurs and therefore no refund is applicable. Your trial automatically ends if you do not add a payment method.

6. Contact

Questions? Email us at support@saleiq.app or visit our Support page.
