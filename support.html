Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to common questions.

How do I install the widget on my website?

Go to Widget Settings in your dashboard, customize your agent, copy the embed code, and paste it before the closing </body> tag on your website. No coding needed.

How are AI words counted?

Only AI-generated responses count toward your word limit — not visitor messages. Your count resets on your billing date each month.

Can I export my leads?

Yes — Pro plan users can export all leads as a CSV from the Leads section of the dashboard.

How do I cancel?

Go to Billing in your dashboard and click "Cancel Plan". Your access continues until end of the billing period.

Can I get a refund?

Yes — full refund within 30 days of your first payment. See our refund policy for details.
