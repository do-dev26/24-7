Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I install the widget on my website?

Go to Widget Settings in your dashboard, customize your agent, then copy the embed code. Paste it before the closing </body> tag on any page of your website. No coding experience needed.

How are "words" counted?

Words are counted from AI-generated responses only (not user messages). Each month your word count resets on your billing date. You can monitor usage in real time from your dashboard Overview.

Can I use SaleIQ on multiple websites?

The Basic plan includes 1 website widget. The Pro plan includes unlimited website widgets. You can manage separate widget configurations for each domain from your dashboard.

How do I export my leads?

Pro plan users can export leads as a CSV file from the Leads section of the dashboard by clicking "Export CSV". Basic plan users can view leads in the dashboard but cannot bulk export.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit using TLS and at rest using AES-256 encryption. We never sell your data. Please review our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel from the Billing section of your dashboard. Your account stays active until the end of the current billing period. For refund eligibility, see our Refund Policy.
